Name: General Anil Chauhan

Born: May 18, 1961

About Him

General Anil Chauhan is a distinguished four-star general in the Indian
Army and currently serves as the 2nd Chief of Defence Staff (CDS) of the Indian Armed Forces
, a position he has held since September 30, 2022. He succeeded General Bipin Rawat, who died in a helicopter crash in December 2021

Early Life and Education

  • Born: May 18, 1961, in Gwana village, Pauri Garhwal district, Uttarakhand (formerly Uttar Pradesh)
  • Family: He comes from a Hindu Garhwali Rajput family. His father, Surendra Singh Chauhan, is a retired Indian Army personnel
  • Education: He completed his schooling at Kendriya Vidyalaya, Fort William, Kolkata, and later graduated from the National Defence Academy (NDA), Khadakwasla, and the Indian Military Academy (IMA), Dehradun
